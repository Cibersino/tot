toT

from Text to Time

toT is a desktop app that turns text into estimated reading time and helps you plan realistic reading workloads.

It combines text import/extraction from text files, images, and PDFs, configurable WPM presets, precise counting, text snapshots, a task editor, and a stopwatch so you can measure, organize, and complete readings with less guesswork.

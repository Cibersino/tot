toT is a desktop app that turns text into estimated reading time and helps you plan realistic reading workloads. It allows you to measure, organize, and complete readings with less guesswork.

It combines text extraction from documents and images, configurable WPM presets, precise counting, text snapshots, a task organizer, a stopwatch with floating window, and reading speed tests.

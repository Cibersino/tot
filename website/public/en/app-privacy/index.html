toT

App Privacy

toT is designed to be local-first for writing, editing, counting, and most day-to-day use. Depending on the features you choose, the app may store or process:

  • current text, saved text snapshots, settings, window state, and task-related data on your device
  • local OCR connection/token state when you choose to use Google OCR

The app may connect to external services only for specific actions, such as opening user-requested links, checking release/update information, or running optional OCR flows.

If you choose to use Google OCR, only the files you explicitly select for OCR are sent to Google for that operation. Those OCR files are not sent to the developers as part of the OCR flow.

For the Google OCR-specific privacy terms, authorization flow, Google account connection, files sent to Google, local token handling, and disconnect behavior, see the dedicated Google OCR Privacy page.

If you want website-only information about hosting, cookies, or Cloudflare-delivered technical cookies, see Website Privacy / Cookies.
